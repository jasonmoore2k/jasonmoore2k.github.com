Moustachery. Noun. Activities or shenanigans most commonly associated with people adorning any number of face ornaments (moustaches) and usually associated with alcohol and/or a lack of pants.

This is an app dedicated to the recognition of:

  • The Movember Foundation and the great work they do in supporting prostate cancer and male mental health initiatives.
  • The moustache aficionado, who appreciates the moustache, what it stands for, what it says about a man and what it can achieve when worn with pride and conviction.

It requires your data connection to connect to the Instagram web service.

This product uses the Instagram API but is not endorsed or certified by Instagram.
