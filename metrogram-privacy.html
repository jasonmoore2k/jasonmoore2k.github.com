privacy policy

MetroGram uses the internet connection on your device to connect only to the web services provided by Instagram. All traffic between MetroGram and Instagram runs over SSL/HTTPS.

MetroGram does not collect or use any of your personal information. When you login (which is optional), MetroGram requests a "token" from the Instagram web services and stores this along with your username in your device's encrypted settings. Your password is never visible to MetroGram and never stored on your device, and the token can be revoked in your account details at https://instagram.com/accounts/manage_access

For more information about the privacy and security policies of the Instagram service, see http://instagram.com/about/legal/privacy

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact me at support@jm2k.me
